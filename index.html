History

Othel Wooden Floors: the parquet’s history that grow. The company, founded by Angelo Othel in Villa della Madonna, Italy, in 1911, is today Italy’s leading parquet manufacturer. Some things change with time, but some others things never change: the company is still a true family business and it is managed personally by the company owners.

The raw materials are chosen by material sourced from forests that are managed correctly from an environmental point of view, to a flooring that upgrades every room and turns your house into a home. Choose Othel products’ quality for a high quality prefinished parquet flooring.

Our Field Of Work

Wood Floor

Choosing Othel wooden floors for your home is prestigious. Wood is a live, exciting, warm material; it has always covered and protected our homes. From the earliest times the skilled cabinetmakers work the wood for those people, who wish an elegant home.

Floor Decorations

Othel takes on this valuable heritage and thanks to its expert staff makes it into art: from the most famous historical houses to the residential apartments, our brand certifies a deep knowledge of the wooden species, our skills in transforming them, our technology and innovation, our special taste, that’s the result of three generations of family’s tradition, well known all around the world

Sculptures

Some of the finest extant examples of early European wood carving are from the Middle Ages in Germany, Russia, Italy and France, where the typical themes of that era were Christian iconography.We at Othel have the skills and creativity to restore and make wood sculptures.
